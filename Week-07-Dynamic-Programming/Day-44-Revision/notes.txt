0/1 Knapsack Pattern Template:

Initialize:
dp = [0] * (target + 1)
dp[0] = 1 or True

For each num:
    for j in range(target, num - 1, -1):
        dp[j] = dp[j] + dp[j - num]   (count version)
        OR
        dp[j] = dp[j] or dp[j - num]  (boolean version)

Important:
Reverse loop ensures element is used only once.
