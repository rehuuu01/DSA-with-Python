Minimum Subset Sum Difference

Idea:
Partition array into two subsets S1 and S2.

Difference = |S1 - S2|

Since:
S2 = total_sum - S1

Difference becomes:
|total_sum - 2*S1|

Goal:
Find subset sum S1 closest to total_sum / 2.

Steps:
1. Compute total_sum.
2. Use subset sum DP to determine possible sums.
3. Check sums from 0 to total_sum//2.
4. Compute minimum difference.
